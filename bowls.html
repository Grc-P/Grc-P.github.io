I love the variety of shapes and sizes they can come in and their infinite uses. Cereal? Soup? Dip? Random clutter? They're good for storing it all.

The Little Green Bowl

I will never make a bowl more beautiful than this. This is my favourite thing I have ever made (except for /that/ green mug). I love how the color on the outside is dark, a mixture of brown and navy and then there is this vibrant green on the inside. The contrast between the two sides is really interesting and I hope to make a lot more in this style, although it is a bit of a pain to do. The bowls must first be dipped in Jupiter brown glaze, before having white poured in the center of the bowl. You have to pour enough white to cover the inside without adding too much that it would cause the glaze to crawl. You also have to make sure that you dont accidentally drip and onto the outside - a mistake I have made with one of my most recent pots (glaze firing pending, images coming soon).

In the future I will aim to use less inside glaze colour than needed and then manually add the remaining glaze by hand - I think this is the best was to control where the glaze goes going forward.
