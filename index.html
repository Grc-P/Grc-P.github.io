Grace's Silly Ceramics

Just a girl enjoying her unemployment

Mugs, bowls and everything in between...

Plans are for the highly skilled, which I am not. I instead see where the clay takes me in the moment, sometimes that mug turns into a bowl, and vice versa. There is no consistency to my forms, nor can I make anything larger than my hand. Welcome along for the ride.
