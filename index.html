Silly Ceramics Co.

Just a girl enjoying her unemployment

Mugs, bowls and everything in between...

Plans are for the highly skilled master potter, which I am not. I instead see where the clay takes me in the moment, sometimes that mug turns into a bowl, and vice versa. There is no consistency to my forms, nor can I make anything larger than my hand.

Using this website as a little record of my journey as a potter. This is not in anyway serious, just a bit of fun. Also there is no built in spell checker for this coding software so enjoy spotting all the mistakes. Welcome along for the ride.
